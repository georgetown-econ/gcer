--- layout: post title: "New Research by GCER Fellow Arik Levinson Featured in Recent WSJ Article." date: 2013-06-20 00:00:00 +UTC ---

A recent article in the Wall Street Journal's on-line edition features new findings by GCER Fellow Arik Levinson. The article examines an NBER working paper by Levinson that questions whether regulation may have caused a drop in California electricity consumption.
