--- layout: post title: "GCER Fellow interviewed in the New York Times and NPR" date: 2014-05-10 12:14:24 --- GCER Fellow Laurent Bouton was interviewed by the New York Times and NPR for their feature on the Senate primaries. Bouton is assistant professor of Economics at Georgetown and studies electoral systems and voter behavior. He has also written a column on the 'gun-control paradox' in VoxEU.
