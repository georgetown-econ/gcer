--- layout: post title: "GCER Fellow Ravallion discusses Piketty's book in Indian Express" date: 2014-05-16 12:14:24 categories: news --- GCER Fellow and Professor of Economics Martin Ravallion wrote a column titled "Reading Piketty in India" in the Indian Express on May 15, 2014. In the article, Ravallion argues that inequality in human capital, rather than physical capital, is India's biggest concern right now.
