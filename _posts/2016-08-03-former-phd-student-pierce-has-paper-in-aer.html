--- layout: post published: true title: Former PhD student Justin Pierce publishes paper in AER date: '2016-08-03 00:00:00' ---

A recent GU graduate of the Department of Economics, Justin Pierce, publishes paper in the most recent American Economic Review. The paper "The Surprisingly Swift Decline of US Manufacturing Employment" links a `sharp drop in US manufacturing employment after 2000 to a change in US trade policy that eliminated potential tariff increases on Chinese imports.' The paper was jointly authored with Peter K. Schott of the Yale School of Management.
