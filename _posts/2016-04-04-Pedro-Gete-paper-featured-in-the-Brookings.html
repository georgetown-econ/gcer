--- layout: post date: "2016-04-04 00:00:00" published: true title: GCER Fellow Pedro Gete's paper featured in the Brookings Hutchins Roundup ---

The Brookings blog of David Wessel selected one of the new papers of Pedro Gete , a GCER Fellow and faculty member in Department of Economics. The paper is about the distributional implications of federal mortgage guarantees, finding that find that removing guarantees from the mortgage market would hurt low- and middle-income households while benefitting high-income households.
