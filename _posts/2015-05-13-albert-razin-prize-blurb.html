Jason Albert received Razin Prize and is shown being congratulated by George Akerlof who delivered the Razin lecture on April 27@@@. Jason received the prize for his dissertation paper titled "Strategic Dynamics of Antibiotic Use and the Evolution of Antibiotic-Resistant Infections".
